iPhone setup
Push notifications on iPhone require the app to be added to your home screen:
1
Open StudyDesk in Safari on your iPhone (not Chrome)
2
Tap the Share button (box with arrow pointing up)
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen" and tap Add
4
Open StudyDesk from your home screen and allow notifications
Requires iOS 16.4 or later.
